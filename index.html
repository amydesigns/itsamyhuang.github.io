my huang

product management

I am a product manager at IBM on the Maximo Asset Performance Management team, where I work closely on Health & Predict - Utilites. I also co-founded Fit.M8, a fitness app where I manage product side and identified opportunities in social fitness and gamification.

ux/ui design

I have done various projects for startups, clubs, and classes. I was a Product Design Intern at Belle Apps, where I created PMBookClub.com. I was the Design Lead for BigRed//Hacks, which organizes the largest annual hackathon at Cornell University, and designed its 2019 website.

digital marketing

I worked as a Digital Marketing Student at Cornell University Press, where I produced weekly email campaigns and maximized open and click rates. I conducted branding efforts and customer discovery research at the startup takk as the Marketing & Business Development Intern.

I graduated from Cornell University, where I majored in Information Science with a concentration in UX Design and a minor in Business. I started at Cornell with uncertainty: I had no idea what I should major in or what I should I do with my life. I knew I was fascinated with people and learning their behaviors and psychology. My introduction to technology was through design, but I quickly found my passion was in creating and working closely with products. Besides my background in PM, design, and marketing, I also have experiences with front-end web development and interactive data visualizations.

I currently reside in Austin, Texas. In the past, I have lived in upstate New York, Michigan, and West Virginia. I have a dog named Maxie and a cat named my Lucy. I enjoy watching (and deeply analyzing) good films and TV shows, and I have long been a history buff. In my free time, I enjoy hiking, traveling, and writing. I hope to publish a novel someday, and I am currently working on one now!
